Information We Collect

Smart Buy Reviews does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, by emailing us). We don't use cookies for tracking, and we don't run analytics scripts that identify individual users.

Hosting & Log Data

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically collect non-personal information such as:

  • IP address

This data is used for security and performance purposes and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Affiliate Links

Smart Buy Reviews participates in the Amazon Associates Programme and other affiliate networks. When you click a product link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate relationships never influence our reviews or recommendations. We only recommend products we genuinely believe offer good value.

External Links

Our site contains links to external websites (retailers, manufacturers, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
