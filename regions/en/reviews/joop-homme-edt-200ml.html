Not sure if it was worth the money at first, but the scent grew on me and lasts a decent amount. After testing JOOP! Homme for three weeks, I can say it's a solid everyday fragrance that punches above its £28 price tag.

First Impressions

The bottle is iconic - that deep red glass with the distinctive shape has been around since 1989, and it still looks premium on a bathroom shelf. When you first spray it, there's a strong initial burst of sweetness that mellows quickly.

This isn't a subtle fragrance. JOOP! Homme announces itself, which could be a pro or con depending on your preference. I'd describe it as confident rather than aggressive.

The Scent Profile

JOOP! Homme is a woody oriental fragrance with some distinctive notes:

  • Top notes: Orange blossom, bergamot - fresh and citrusy opening
  • Heart notes: Jasmine, heliotrope, cinnamon - the sweet, warm character
  • Base notes: Tonka bean, sandalwood, honey - what lingers after a few hours

The honey and tonka bean are what make this fragrance memorable. It's sweet without being cloying, with enough woody depth to keep it masculine.

Longevity and Projection

Here's where JOOP! Homme performs well for the price. I consistently got 6-8 hours of wear, with the first 3-4 hours having noticeable projection. After that, it becomes more of a skin scent.

Two sprays on the neck and one on the wrist was enough for a full day. More than that risks being overpowering in close quarters.

Who Is This For?

JOOP! Homme works best for:

  • Evening events and nights out
  • Autumn and winter months (too heavy for summer)
  • Men who prefer sweeter, warmer fragrances
  • Anyone wanting a distinctive signature scent without spending £100+

What We Liked

  • Excellent value at £28 for 200ml
  • Distinctive, memorable scent
  • Good 6-8 hour longevity
  • Iconic bottle design

What Could Be Better

  • Too heavy for hot weather
  • Sweetness may not suit everyone
  • Strong opening might overwhelm

The Verdict

JOOP! Homme isn't trying to be a versatile all-rounder. It's a bold, sweet, woody fragrance that works brilliantly for evenings and cooler months. At £28 for a 200ml bottle, it's exceptional value.

If you prefer fresh, clean fragrances, this isn't for you. But if you want something distinctive that gets compliments and lasts all evening, JOOP! Homme delivers.
