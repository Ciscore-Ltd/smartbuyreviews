JOOP! Homme - Eau de Toilette for Men - Woody Fragrance with Notes of Orange Blossom, Tonka Bean Accord and Honey - Medium Longevity

Smells like my grandad used to wear, so that's a good thing.

Overview

I picked up this JOOP! Homme Eau de Toilette for a tenner under £30 on Amazon, and it's a proper blast from the past. It's got that classic woody vibe with sweet honey and tonka bean hitting you first, then orange blossom softening it up. Sprayed it on after a shower and it feels comforting, like slipping into an old favourite jumper. Not too modern or trendy, but dead reliable for everyday wear or a night out if you're not chasing the latest hype.

I've been testing it over a week now – two sprays on the neck and wrists – and it hangs around for about 4-5 hours, which matches the 'medium longevity' they bang on about. Price-wise at £27 for 200ml, it's a bargain compared to some of the designer stuff that's double the cost and half as characterful.

Key Features

  • • Woody base with standout notes of orange blossom, tonka bean accord, and honey – sweet but not cloying.
  • • Eau de Toilette concentration for medium projection and 4-6 hour wear time.
  • • 200ml bottle – lasts ages if you're not heavy-handed.
  • • Classic 80s-inspired men's fragrance, great for mature tastes or nostalgia seekers.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Proper nostalgic scent – honey and tonka make it unique and cosy.
  • Decent value at £27 for a big bottle.
  • Not overpowering, blends well in a crowd.
  • Compliments from older mates who've smelt it before.

Cons

  • A bit dated – might not suit under-30s or clubbing.
  • Longevity is medium at best; needs re-spraying midday.
  • Sweetness could turn sickly in hot weather.

Verdict

If you're after a solid, affordable woody frag with a touch of sweetness and don't mind a retro edge, JOOP! Homme is spot on. It's not going to blow your socks off like some niche stuff, but for the price and the smiles it brings, I'd recommend it. Proper grandad-approved!
