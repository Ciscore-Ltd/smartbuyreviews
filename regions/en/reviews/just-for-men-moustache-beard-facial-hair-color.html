Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye Review: A Quick Fix for Grey Hairs

Grey hairs disappeared in minutes like magic. I picked up the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye (M45) on a whim while browsing Amazon, and for £8.67, I wasn’t expecting miracles. But honestly, I’m pretty chuffed with how it turned out. If you’re looking to cover up those pesky greys in your beard or moustache and want something quick and easy, this might just do the trick.

Overview: What’s This Dye All About?

This product from Just for Men is designed specifically for facial hair, promising to zap grey hairs in just 5 minutes while giving your beard or moustache a thicker, fuller appearance. The shade I went for is Dark Brown (M45), which seemed a close match to my natural colour. The packaging warns that the colour might vary slightly from what’s shown, but I didn’t notice any major differences. It’s marketed as a fast, no-mess solution for blokes who want to keep their facial hair looking sharp without spending ages on it.

Features: What Stands Out?

  • Quick Application: True to the claim, it only takes 5 minutes to work. Mix the colour base and developer, slap it on with the included brush, wait, and rinse. Job done.
  • Targeted for Facial Hair: Unlike regular hair dye, this is formulated for beards and moustaches, so it’s less harsh on the skin (though I’d still recommend a patch test).
  • Natural-Looking Colour: The dark brown blended well with my natural shade, no weird orange or overly dark patches.
  • Brush Included: Comes with a small brush for precise application, which is handy for avoiding a mess.

Pros and Cons: The Good and the Not-So-Good

Pros

  • Super quick to apply and rinse off.
  • Covers grey hairs effectively with a natural finish.
  • Doesn’t stain skin if you’re careful with the brush.
  • One box lasts a few applications if you’ve got a shorter beard.

Cons

  • Smell is a bit strong during application, though it fades after rinsing.
  • Colour starts to fade after a couple of weeks, so you’ll need to reapply.
  • Can be a faff to mix if you’re in a rush.

Verdict: Worth the £8.67?

Overall, I’m happy with the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye. It’s not perfect – the fading after a fortnight means it’s not a set-and-forget solution, and the chemical whiff isn’t pleasant. But for a quick, easy way to cover greys and make your facial hair look a bit fuller, it does the job nicely. At £8.67, it’s not the cheapest option out there, but the results are decent enough to justify the price for a one-off or occasional use. If you’re on the fence, I’d say give it a go – just don’t expect miracles to last forever.
