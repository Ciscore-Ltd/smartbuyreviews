Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye Review: Does It Really Eliminate Grey?

Been looking forward to trying this for ages, finally got around to using it. As someone who's been battling the creeping grey in my beard for a while now, I picked up the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye (M45) hoping for a quick fix. Priced at £8.67, it’s not exactly cheap for a small box of dye, but if it delivers on the promise of banishing grey in just 5 minutes and giving a thicker, fuller look, I’m all ears. Here’s my honest take after testing it out.

Overview: What’s in the Box?

This product from Just for Men is specifically designed for facial hair, targeting moustaches and beards to cover grey hairs with a dark brown shade. The packaging warns that the colour might vary slightly (mine looked pretty much as expected), and it’s marketed as a fast-acting solution—5 minutes and you’re done. Inside, you get a colour base, a developer, a mixing tray, and a small brush for application. There’s also a pair of gloves, which is handy because this stuff can stain like nobody’s business. It’s meant to blend with your natural colour for a subtle result, not a harsh, obvious dye job.

Features and Experience

Applying it was straightforward enough. You mix the base and developer in the tray (it’s a bit fiddly if you’ve got big hands like me), then use the brush to slap it on. I focused on the greyer patches of my beard and moustache, and true to the claim, it only took 5 minutes before I rinsed it off. The colour took well—my grey hairs were completely gone, and the dark brown shade looked fairly natural, though a tad darker than I expected. It did make my beard look a bit fuller, probably because the colour evened everything out. One thing to note: it’s got a strong chemical smell during application, so open a window if you can.

It’s supposed to last until the grey grows back, and after a couple of weeks, I can say it holds up decently, though you’ll need to touch up the roots as they appear. My skin didn’t react badly, but I’ve heard some blokes get irritation, so do a patch test first if you’re worried.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Really does cover grey in 5 minutes—super quick.
  • Gives a fuller, thicker look to the beard.
  • Easy to apply with the included brush and tray.
  • Colour lasts a decent amount of time before needing a touch-up.

Cons

  • Strong chemical smell during application.
  • Shade can come out a bit darker than expected.
  • Touch-ups are needed every few weeks as grey grows back.
  • Bit pricey for the amount you get in the box.

Verdict

Overall, I’m pretty chuffed with the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye. It does exactly what it says on the tin—gets rid of grey fast and adds a bit of oomph to your facial hair. Yeah, the smell’s a bit much, and the shade was slightly darker than I’d have liked, but it’s a small price to pay for looking a few years younger. At £8.67, it’s not the cheapest option out there, but it’s worth it for the ease and results. If you’re on the fence about covering up those greys, I’d say give this a go—just be prepared for a touch-up routine. Definitely a solid choice for most blokes.
