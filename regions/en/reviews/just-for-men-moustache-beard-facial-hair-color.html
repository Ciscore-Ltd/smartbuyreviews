Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye Review: Does It Really Eliminate Grey?

Bit surprised it's so easy to apply, didn't think that was possible. I picked up the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye (M45) for £8.67 on Amazon, hoping to banish the grey hairs creeping into my facial fuzz. As a bloke in his 40s, I’m not ready to look like Father Christmas just yet, so I thought I’d give this a go. Spoiler: it’s not half bad for the price, but there are a few things you should know before slapping it on.

Overview: What’s the Deal with Just for Men M45?

This product promises to get rid of grey hairs in just 5 minutes while giving your moustache and beard a thicker, fuller appearance. It’s specifically designed for facial hair, which is coarser than the stuff on your head, so it’s meant to penetrate better than regular hair dye. The shade I went for is Dark Brown (M45), and it comes in a small box with a colour base, developer, mixing tray, and a little brush for application. The packaging warns that the colour may vary slightly, but I reckon it matched my natural tone pretty well.

Features: What Stands Out?

  • Quick Application: They’re not joking about the 5-minute thing. Mix it up, brush it on, wait a bit, and rinse. Job done.
  • Targeted Formula: It’s made for facial hair, so it doesn’t feel as harsh as some head hair dyes I’ve tried in the past.
  • Natural-Looking Colour: The Dark Brown shade blended well with my existing hair, no weird orange or jet-black vibes here.
  • Compact Kit: Comes with everything you need, though the brush is a bit fiddly if you’ve got a thick beard.

Pros and Cons: The Good and the Not-So-Good

Pros

  • Super easy to use, even for a first-timer like me.
  • Colour looks natural and covers grey effectively.
  • Decent value at £8.67 for a few applications if you’re just doing a moustache.

Cons

  • Doesn’t really make hair look thicker or fuller as claimed.
  • Bit of a strong chemical smell while applying, though it fades after rinsing.
  • Colour starts to fade after a couple of weeks, so it’s not a long-term fix.

Verdict: Worth the £8.67?

Overall, I’m pretty chuffed with the Just for Men Moustache & Beard Dark Brown Dye. It does what it says on the tin—gets rid of grey hairs quickly and leaves a natural-looking colour. However, don’t expect miracles in the ‘thicker and fuller’ department; it’s more of a cosmetic cover-up than a magic potion. The fade after a fortnight means you’ll need to reapply regularly, but for the price, it’s a decent quick fix if you’ve got a date or a Zoom call and want to look a bit sharper. I’d give it a solid 4 out of 5—knocking off a point for the exaggerated claims and slight fade. If you’re on the fence, it’s worth a punt for under a tenner.
