BOSS Bottled Night - Eau de Toilette for Him - Woody Fragrance with Notes of Birch Leaf, Cardamom and Musk - Medium Longevity

Smells better on me than in the bottle, which is weird. When I first got my hands on the BOSS Bottled Night, I was a bit skeptical about how it would turn out. But lo and behold, once it settled on my skin, it transformed into something quite lovely. Let's dive into the nitty-gritty of this £31 eau de toilette and see if it's worth your quid.

Key Features

The BOSS Bottled Night is a woody fragrance that boasts a blend of birch leaf, cardamom, and musk. Priced at £31, it's positioned as a medium longevity eau de toilette, which means you'll need to reapply if you're out for the entire day. The bottle itself is quite iconic - sleek and stylish, it's a nice addition to any dresser.

The top notes kick off with the fresh and green scent of birch leaf, which gives it a very natural and outdoor feel. Then, the middle notes introduce a bit of spice with cardamom, which adds depth and warmth to the fragrance. Finally, the base notes of musk provide a sensual and lasting scent that lingers on the skin.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Unique scent combination that evolves nicely on the skin.
  • Affordable at £31, making it accessible for those on a budget.
  • The bottle design is both modern and classy, making it a great display piece.
  • The birch leaf and cardamom notes provide a refreshing and spicy twist.

Cons:

  • Medium longevity means you'll need to reapply during the day.
  • Some may find the musk note a bit too strong or lingering.
  • The fragrance might not be as bold or long-lasting as other BOSS offerings.

Verdict

Overall, the BOSS Bottled Night is a decent choice for those looking for a woody fragrance with a twist. The initial scepticism I had about the scent in the bottle vanished once it hit my skin, which is a testament to how well it develops over time. At £31, it's not going to break the bank, and the stylish bottle adds a touch of class to your collection.

However, if you're after something that lasts all day without needing a top-up, you might want to consider a different option. The musk base note might also be a bit overpowering for some, but if you're into that sort of thing, you'll be well pleased.

In the end, if you're looking for a fragrance that offers a unique scent experience and you don't mind reapplying throughout the day, the BOSS Bottled Night is worth a shot. It's a bit of a grower, and once it settles on your skin, you might find yourself pleasantly surprised.
